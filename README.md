
# ML & OR Project Portfolio

This repository showcases a collection of machine learning and operations research projects spanning **optimization, simulation, computer vision, natural language processing, and decision-making models**.
Projects are listed in **reverse chronological order (most recent → least recent)**.

---

## **Columbia Evening Shuttle (Via) Simulation Project**

*Columbia Evening Shuttle (Via) Simulation Project.docx*

A stochastic, discrete-event simulation evaluating **door-to-door, corner-based, and hybrid routing policies** for a campus night shuttle system, demonstrating system-wide reductions in wait and travel times under **capacity and SLA constraints**.

---

## **Vision Transformers with Relative Positional Encodings (RPEs)**

*Final CIFAR-10 Vision_Transformer.ipynb · Final MNIST Vision_Transformer.ipynb · Improving efficient Vision Transformers with RPEs.pdf*

An empirical study on improving the **efficiency of Vision Transformers** by integrating relative positional encodings, evaluated on **MNIST and CIFAR-10** benchmarks.

---

## **NBA Game Rescheduling Optimization**

*Optimization_Project_NBA_Rescheduling_Problem_Code.ipynb · Project_NBA_Rescheduling_Problem.pdf*

An operations research project addressing **large-scale schedule disruptions**, using optimization models to minimize **travel distance, rest imbalance, and conflict violations** under real-world constraints.

---

## **Healthcare Resource Allocation & Scheduling Optimization**

*Optimization_Project_Healthcare_Resource_Allocation_and_Scheduling_Optimization_Problem.ipynb*

A mathematical optimization project modeling **healthcare resource allocation and scheduling** under capacity and operational constraints, aimed at improving system efficiency and service coverage.

## **Ancient Yi Script Similarity Detection with Siamese Networks**

*ancient-yi-siamese-network-project.ipynb*

Applied **few-shot learning and Siamese neural networks** to identify variant characters in the ancient **Nuosu Yi script**, focusing on visual similarity across regional glyph variations.
